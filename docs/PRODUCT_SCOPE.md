# Product Scope

## Release Goal

Deliver a closed-pilot nutrition platform with strong flows for both nutritionists and patients.

## In Scope

- nutritionist backoffice
- patient onboarding and personal app access
- patient dashboard and tracking
- nutrition plans and daily meals
- meal photo upload and AI analysis
- AI plan generation
- human chat
- nutritionist and patient copilots
- patient risk radar
- meal swap coach
- AI food purchase list for Chile and Spain

## Out Of Scope For The First Release

- open marketplace or public nutritionist self-serve SaaS
- autonomous clinical decision making without human review
- automatic outbound messaging without nutritionist review
- native grocery checkout

## Pilot Success Criteria

- nutritionist can onboard a patient and manage them safely
- patient can follow the plan through the mobile-oriented app
- AI features improve adherence instead of adding friction
- uploads and grocery flows degrade gracefully under external failures
